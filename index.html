Welcome to RSI Uganda Ltd

We are a non-profit run by refugees supporting education, large-scale climate-smart farming, livestock (goats & cows) and community development in Uganda.

Learn about our flagship project

Agriculture & Livestock

We help acquire leased land for large-scale farming, train farmers on climate-smart agriculture, and support livestock projects (goats, cows, poultry).

  • Large-scale farm setup
  • Livestock husbandry & veterinary support
  • Market linkages & value addition

Education & Skills

Literacy, numeracy, vocational training and entrepreneurship courses for refugees and host communities.

  • Vocational workshops
  • Financial literacy & small business support
  • Informal education for children

Social Development

Supporting Village Savings & Loan Associations (VSLAs), women & youth groups, leadership and psychosocial support.

Climate Change & Resilience

We promote drought-resistant crops, agroforestry, water harvesting and renewable energy (biogas & solar) to increase resilience.

Flagship Project — Refugee Farming & Empowerment (USD 100,000)

Two-year pilot to support refugees with land leases, farm setup, livestock, training and market access.

  • Land lease & farm setup — USD 20,000
  • Inputs & climate-smart kits — USD 15,000
  • Livestock (goats, cows, poultry) — USD 15,000
  • Training & education — USD 20,000
  • Climate initiatives & M&E — USD 20,000
  • Administration & contingency — USD 10,000
